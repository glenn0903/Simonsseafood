CRABS

Price per kilo: Php 420

Crabs are decapod crustaceans of the infraorder Brachyura, which typically have a very short projecting "tail", usually
hidden entirely under the thorax.

Best to cook as: mix with ginataan, adobo

SQUID

Price per kilo: Php 400

A squid is any of more than 300 species of 10-armed mollusks constituting the cephalopod order Teuthoidea (or Teuthida).
They are found in coastal and as well as oceanic waters.

Best to cook as: adobo, gisado, calamares

BANGUS

Price per kilo: Php 230

Milkfish, which is locally known as bangus and scientifically named as Chanos chanos, is the Philippines' national fish. Being one of the most popularly cultured fishes in the country, milkfish can be raised through brackish water, marine, and freshwater culture environments.

Best to cook as: prito, paksiw, relyenong bangus, sugba, sinigang, tinola, marinated bangus
